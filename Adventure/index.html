    I got off the boat in the early hours of the morning. I had to wait to break my fast as any reputable inns would be closed at this time. As I waited at the docks, the sun began to rise, and along with it, the denizens of Kings Landing. The merchants were the first to get up, I saw them talking with the sailors, buying the silks and pearls from the Free Cities, and selling pottery and jewelry.

  • Riding with the sun to the Westerlands image 2

    2 September 2022

    I got into Lannisport right as the day was ending. I had ridden with the sun all day and both he and I would rest as the sky darkened. Or so I thought. Lannisport is more active at night than during the day it seems. The streets come to life as the lanterns are lit and the people come out to enjoy the coolness of night. I ended up being far too tired to join them, but as I drifted off to sleep I heard the songs of bards and the laughs of glee all around me
